CPSC 510 - Artificial Intelligence I

This course provided infromation in regards to AI, specifically in terms of the math, programming, and social implementations of it. Throughout the course a big topic was the different search algorithms that could be used, the search alrotighms that were discussed including:

- Breadth First Search (BFS)
- Depth First Search (DFS)
- Uniform Cost Search (UCS)
- A* Search

We also discussed how to complete these tasks by hand, using maps and graphs to work through the different search algorithms. Methods such as MiniMax and Alpha Beta pruning were explains to help the search agents make decisions and find the most efficient paths. There was also discussion of the implementations of AI and how it is being used, specifically in artificial image creation.
